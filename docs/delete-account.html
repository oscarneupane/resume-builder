Delete Your Account & Data

App: ApplyMate  ·  Developer: Oscar Neupane

ApplyMate lets you permanently delete your account and all associated data at any time. You can do this directly inside the app, or by contacting us.

Delete in the app (recommended)

  1. Open ApplyMate and sign in.
  2. Go to Settings → Data & Privacy.
  3. Tap Delete account and confirm.

This immediately and permanently removes your account and all of your stored data.

Delete by request

If you cannot access the app, email us from the address on your account and ask for deletion:

  • Email: monikagharti3439@gmail.com
  • Subject: ApplyMate account deletion

We will process the request and confirm once your account and data have been deleted.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and sign-in identity (email / Google authentication).
  • Your name, email, and profile details.
  • All content you created: resumes, cover letters, job applications, interview notes, and uploaded files.

All of the above are permanently deleted with no retention period. We do not keep backups of deleted user content beyond the time technically required to remove it from active and backup systems (no longer than 30 days). We never sell your data or use your content to train AI models.
