ApplyMate

Privacy Policy

Effective 24 June 2026

Overview

ApplyMate is owned and operated by Oscar Neupane. This policy explains how ApplyMate collects, uses, stores, and protects information when you use the app or website.

Information We Collect

We may collect account details such as name, username, email address, phone number, address, and Google profile information approved during sign in. We also process resumes, cover letters, job posts, uploaded files, notes, and AI-generated outputs that you choose to create or save.

How We Use Information

We use information to provide app features, save documents, run AI generation, analyze ATS fit, prepare interview content, improve reliability, prevent abuse, and support subscriptions or billing where available.

AI Processing

When you use AI features, relevant user-provided content may be sent to configured AI providers such as Claude API or OpenAI API to generate the requested result. ApplyMate does not sell resume content and does not use user documents to train its own AI model.

Google Authentication

Google sign in is used for account creation and authentication. ApplyMate requests basic scopes such as email, profile, and openid so the app can identify the signed-in user.

Storage And Security

ApplyMate uses Supabase for authentication, database storage, and private file storage. Access policies are designed so users can access only their own records. API keys for AI providers are intended to stay on backend functions, not inside the public mobile app.

Your Choices

You can update or delete documents from the app. You may request account or data deletion by contacting the project owner.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised through the project repository at github.com/oscarneupane/resume-builder.
