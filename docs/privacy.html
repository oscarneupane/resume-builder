Privacy Policy

Effective 18 June 2026

Overview

ApplyMate ("we", "us", "our") is owned and operated by ApplyMate. This Privacy Policy explains what we collect, why, and how we handle it when you use the app.

Information we collect

Account details you provide (name, username, email). Content you create or upload — resumes, cover letters, job applications, and files you add to Materials. Basic usage information needed to operate features (for example, AI generation counts used for rate limiting). If you sign in with Google, we receive your basic profile (name, email) via the scopes you approve.

How we use your information

To provide the app’s features: building documents, AI generation, ATS checks, storage, and subscriptions. We use your content only to deliver the result you asked for. We do not sell your data, and we do not use your resume content to train AI models.

AI processing

When you use an AI feature, the relevant content is sent to our AI provider (Google’s Gemini API) solely to generate your result. We log only usage counts for rate limiting — not the content of your requests.

Storage & security

Your data is stored with our backend provider (Supabase) and protected with row-level security so you can only access your own data. Files are kept in per-user private storage. Sensitive keys are never stored in the app.

Your choices & rights

You can edit or delete your documents at any time. You can export your data and permanently delete your account from Settings, which removes your associated records.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Contact ApplyMate at support@applymate.app.
